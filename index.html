Some things about how do i test

I am young, inquisitive and a very sincere person. Every day I struggle with software, strive to keep it better and better. Error free. Easy to use. So i start testing by getting to know the reason for creating the software. Then I check the technology in which the application was created. And then I start planning. And when i finished it, i can finally start testing.

I believe that doing your job without a plan is pointless. As a result of this, some elements of (in my case) testing are often unnecessarily repeated or even poorly performed. I'm testing manually and writing automatic tests. In some cases it is very good to repeat an activity while looking for different kinds of errors. However, most often it is enough to write a good automatic test once and then support it.
